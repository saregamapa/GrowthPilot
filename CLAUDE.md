# 🧠 PROJECT: 3D WEBSITE — AI SOCIAL MEDIA MARKETING AGENCY

## 🎯 OBJECTIVE
Build a visually stunning, high-conversion, modern 3D website for an AI-powered social media marketing agency serving professionals across industries.

This is NOT a template website.
This should feel like a premium product (similar quality to Apple, Stripe, or top Webflow sites).

---

## 🏷️ BRAND CONTEXT

**Brand Name:** BrandElevate AI (or similar premium name)

**Positioning:**
AI-powered social media marketing & personal branding agency helping professionals attract clients through content, automation, and authority building.

**Target Audience:**
- Real estate agents & brokers
- Mortgage lenders
- Lawyers & accountants
- Doctors & healthcare professionals
- Coaches & educators
- Local business owners (salons, restaurants, agencies)

---

## 🧩 CORE GOAL

Convert visitors into leads by:
- Building trust instantly
- Showing transformation (before → after)
- Demonstrating authority
- Driving users to book a strategy call

---

## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM

- Theme: LIGHT (no dark UI)
- Style: Premium, minimal, futuristic
- Colors: White, soft blue, light purple, subtle gold accents
- Effects:
  - Glassmorphism
  - Soft shadows
  - Floating UI elements
  - Smooth gradients

---

## 🧊 3D & ANIMATION REQUIREMENTS

### Hero Section (MANDATORY)
- Add 3D animated background:
  - Floating social media icons
  - Content cards
  - Analytics dashboards
- Subtle parallax or motion effect
- Optional: embed video (Kling-generated)

### Technologies (choose best fit):
- Three.js OR
- CSS 3D transforms OR
- Lottie animations

### Motion:
- Smooth scrolling
- Fade-in + slide-up animations
- Hover interactions (lift, glow, scale)

Respect:
- `prefers-reduced-motion`
