FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started. Still have questions? We’ll walk you through everything on a strategy call.

Schedule a Call
How long before I see real results?
Most clients see measurable engagement growth within the first 30 days. Significant lead generation typically kicks in between 60–90 days as your audience builds trust in your brand. Authority-level results — speaking invitations, media features, inbound deals — usually emerge after 4–6 months of consistent presence.
Which social platforms do you manage?
We work across Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, TikTok, YouTube, X (Twitter), Pinterest, and WhatsApp Business. Your strategy session will identify which 2–4 platforms deliver the highest ROI for your specific profession and target audience.
Do you create the content or do I need to be involved?
We handle 90% of the heavy lifting — writing, design, scheduling, and engagement strategy. Your only requirement is a brief monthly onboarding call (30 minutes) so we can stay aligned with your messaging and any key updates in your business. You review and approve before anything goes live.
Do you work with my specific industry?
Absolutely. We have active clients in real estate, mortgage lending, law, medicine, financial advising, coaching, consulting, restaurants, salons, and general local business. Our team researches your industry's compliance requirements, competitive landscape, and audience behaviour before writing a single word.
What’s included in the strategy session?
A 30-minute session with a senior strategist. We'll audit your current social presence, benchmark you against top performers in your market, identify your two or three biggest growth levers, and outline a custom 90-day roadmap — with no obligation to move forward.
Is there a minimum contract length?
No long-term contracts. We work on a rolling month-to-month basis because we believe our results should earn your business every single month. Most clients stay for 12+ months because they see consistent ROI, not because they're locked in.
How do you measure success?
Every client receives a monthly performance dashboard covering follower growth, engagement rate, reach, inbound lead volume, and content performance by post. We set clear KPIs during onboarding and hold ourselves accountable to them — if we fall short, we tell you why and what we're doing to fix it.
What if I'm starting from zero followers?
Many of our best success stories started from scratch. A small, engaged audience consistently outperforms a large, disengaged one. We focus on building real community with potential clients — not vanity metrics — so even new accounts start generating leads within the first few months.
