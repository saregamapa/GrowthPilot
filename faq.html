General

BrokerBoost is a full-service social media marketing agency built specifically for professionals in high-trust industries. We handle everything from brand strategy and content creation to lead generation funnels and paid advertising — so you can focus on serving clients while we fill your pipeline. We're not a software tool or a generic agency. We're a specialized team that understands the compliance requirements, credibility standards, and lead quality expectations of industries like real estate, law, medicine, mortgage, and financial services.
We work with professionals who have valuable expertise but aren't getting the online visibility they deserve. Our typical client is a real estate agent, mortgage broker, lawyer, doctor, accountant, coach, or local business owner with annual revenue of $250K+ who knows their social media presence isn't matching the quality of their actual service. We also work with brokerage firms, law firms, and multi-location businesses looking to build brand equity across their team's individual profiles.
Three things set us apart. First, we specialize exclusively in regulated, high-trust industries — we understand RESPA, FINRA, and healthcare advertising restrictions that generic agencies get wrong. Second, every client gets a dedicated senior strategist, not a junior account coordinator. Third, we work month-to-month because we believe results — not contracts — should keep clients. Most agencies lock you in for 6–12 months; we earn your business every single month.
Our core team is based in the United States, with strategists in Austin, New York, and Miami. We work with clients across the US, Canada, UK, and Australia. Strategy sessions are conducted over video call, and our systems work across all time zones. Compliance-sensitive content is always reviewed against the regulatory framework specific to your country and jurisdiction.

Most professionals in regulated industries see the highest ROI from LinkedIn and Instagram, though this varies significantly by niche and target audience.

Timeline & Results

Timelines vary by starting point, but here's what most clients experience: In the first 30 days, you'll see measurable improvements in profile quality, content consistency, and initial engagement metrics. Between days 31–60, audience growth accelerates and inbound messages typically start increasing. By day 60–90, a steady flow of qualified leads is usually established. Authority-level results — media mentions, speaking invitations, significant deal flow attributed to social — typically emerge after 4–6 months of consistent, high-quality presence.
We've launched profiles from zero to 5,000+ engaged followers in 90 days — and it's one of our favorite challenges. Starting fresh means no bad content to undo, no outdated branding to work around. We build your audience strategically from day one, targeting the exact profile of your ideal client using hashtag strategy, collaboration tactics, and targeted content. For professionals starting from scratch, we also recommend a modest paid advertising budget ($300–$500/month) to accelerate the early follower growth phase.
Every client receives a monthly performance dashboard that tracks: follower growth (net and gross), engagement rate, reach and impressions, inbound DM and inquiry volume, lead capture conversions, and paid campaign ROAS (if applicable). We set agreed-upon KPIs during your onboarding call and hold ourselves accountable to them in monthly reviews. If a metric is underperforming, we explain why and what we're changing — not just show you a pretty graph.
Week 1 is discovery and strategy — your brand audit, competitor analysis, ICP workshop, and 90-day roadmap. Weeks 2–3 are production — we build your first 30-day content library, refresh your profiles, and set up your approval workflow. Week 4 is systems — lead capture, email sequences, analytics, and ad accounts. By day 28, your channels are live, scheduled, and running. Day 30 is your first check-in call where we review early metrics and confirm you're happy with the direction.

Technical

We never ask for personal passwords. Instead, we use official platform tools designed for agency access: Facebook Business Manager, LinkedIn Company Page admin access, and similar role-based permissions. For scheduling and analytics, we use enterprise-grade tools where you can grant specific permissions without sharing login credentials. You can revoke our access at any time with one click. We take account security seriously — our team undergoes security training and we never store platform credentials.
This is one of our core competencies. Before writing a single word of content, we review the regulatory environment for your specific profession and jurisdiction — FINRA, RESPA, state bar advertising rules, HIPAA considerations, FTC disclosure requirements, and more. We have compliance playbooks for real estate, mortgage, legal, medical, and financial services. Every piece of content is reviewed against these standards before entering your approval queue. We also flag any claims in your existing content that could create regulatory exposure.
We work well alongside existing teams. We can take over full management, work in a hybrid model (e.g., we handle strategy and content production while your team handles engagement), or serve as a strategic advisor to your in-house manager. We've also onboarded into clients' existing tool stacks — from Buffer to Hootsuite to Sprout Social. The key is alignment on voice and process, which we establish in the first two weeks regardless of the arrangement.
