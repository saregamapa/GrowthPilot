Questions About Pricing

Everything You Need to Know Before Investing

Still have questions? Our team will walk you through every detail on a free strategy call — no pressure, no obligation.

Schedule a Call
Are there setup fees?
No setup fees, ever. The price you see on our pricing page is the only price you pay. We invest our own time and resources in your onboarding because we're confident in our ability to generate results that justify our fee many times over.
Can I change plans later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade at the start of any new billing month. Upgrading mid-month is prorated. If you're unsure which plan to start with, our strategy session recommendation will make it clear — we'd rather you start at the right level than over-invest from day one.
What's included in ad spend?
Ad spend is a separate budget paid directly to the platforms (Meta, LinkedIn, etc.) and is not included in your plan pricing. Our fee covers campaign strategy, ad creative, setup, optimization, and reporting. We'll recommend an appropriate monthly ad budget based on your goals — typically $300–$1,500/month to start.
Do you offer refunds?
We work month-to-month, so you're never locked in. We don't offer partial-month refunds for cancellations mid-cycle, but we will always complete any paid month of service in full.
How does cancellation work?
Cancel any time by contacting your dedicated strategist before your next billing date. There are no cancellation fees, no penalties, and no awkward conversations. We'll offboard you professionally and hand over all the content assets we've created for you.
Do you provide invoices for business expense claims?
Yes. All subscription payments through Stripe include an automatic invoice sent to your billing email. We can also provide a formal invoice letter on request for accountants who need additional documentation. Your subscription is a legitimate business marketing expense.
